cissp_mnemonics

co-authored with: OpenAI's ChatGPT

1. Bell-LaPadula Model Focus: Maintaining confidentiality and controlling access based on security clearances. Mnemonic: "Be Like Pigeons, Quietly Confiding."
2. Biba Model Focus: Ensuring data integrity by preventing unauthorized data modification. Mnemonic: "Be Incredible, Block Alterations."
3. Clark-Wilson Model Focus: Data integrity through well-defined transactions. Mnemonic: "Clean Waters, Intact Ships."
4. Brewer and Nash Model (The Chinese Wall) Focus: Preventing conflict of interest in access control. Mnemonic: "Build New, Challenging Walls."
5. Non-Interference Model Focus: Preventing actions at a high security level from affecting lower levels. Mnemonic: "Never Intrude, Maintain Separation."
6. Take-Grant Model Focus: Describing how rights can be transferred or revoked between subjects. Mnemonic: "Take Gently, Give Respectfully."
